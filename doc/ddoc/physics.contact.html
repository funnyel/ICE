struct Contact ¶
Stores data about a contact between two physics bodies.
PhysicsBody body_a [public] ¶
First body involved in the contact.
PhysicsBody body_b [public] ¶
Second body involved in the contact.
Vector2f contact_normal [public] ¶
Normal of the contact. Contact normal is the direction we must move body_b (and opposite of the direction we must move body_a) to resolve the contact.
float penetration [public] ¶
Greatest depth of interpenetration between the bodies.
bool resolved [public] ¶
If true, this contact is already resolved - do not resolve it further.
void swap_bodies() [package] ¶
Swap the bodies in the contact, and flip the direction of the contact normal.
void resolve_penetration(out Vector2f change_a, out Vector2f change_b) [package] ¶
Resolve penetration between the bodies by changing their positions.
No resolution is done if both bodies have infinite mass. This might change if bugs arise.

Parameters:

change_aVector to write position change of the first body to.
change_bVector to write position change of the second body to.
void collision_response() [package] ¶
Handle collision response to the contact (e.g. bodies bouncing away).
float desired_delta_velocity() [package] ¶
Return desired change of velocity of the contact (total of both bodies) for collision response.
real inverse_mass_total() [package] ¶
Return sum of inverse masses of bodies involved in this contact.
