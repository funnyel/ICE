class GLVideoDriver : VideoDriver [abstract] ¶
OpenGL (currently a mix of 1.x and 2.x, planning to be purely 2.x) based video driver.
Most of the actual drawing is done by GLRenderer, GLVideoDriver basically manages other GL video classes.
